This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") describes the treatment of information that is provided by or collected through your use of websites (including  BixBeta.com), applications, or services  operated by BixBeta Inc.

When you use the Site, we want to make sure you understand how we collect, use, share and keep information about you and the choices you have when you use the Site. By using or accessing the Site, you are accepting the terms of this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, disclosure and protection of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. 


“BixBeta”, “we”, “us” or “our” means BixBeta Inc., a Delaware corporation, and all its related, affiliated or subsidiary companies.“You” and “your”’ means the natural person using the Site or an entity using the Site and any natural person using the Site and acting on the entity’s behalf.

1. Types of information we collect

‍

We collect two types of information through the Site – personal information and anonymous information.

"Personal information" refers to data that directly or indirectly identifies a specific person, such as information submitted when requesting a demo of a product, inquiring about employment, or registering for the application. Some examples of Personal Information include your name, mailing address, email, phone number, birthdate, and account details.

‍

"Anonymous information" means data that cannot be linked to, or reasonably used to identify, an individual or business entity. This type of information includes details about your web browser, data collected through tracking methods (refer to the "Online Tracking and Advertising" section for more information on our utilization of tracking methods), and consolidated or anonymized data.

‍

Listed below is an extensive categorization of the Personal Information that we may gather or create while you use the Site, along with relevant explanations and examples:

  • Personal Identifiers:  This includes first and last name, previous name, address, telephone number, unique personal identifier, and signature.
  • Device and Online Identifiers and Related Information:  This includes online identifiers, Internet Protocol (IP) address, email address, account user name, mobile/ wireless number, and other device information.
  • Purchase History:  This includes your purchase history or tendencies.
  • Internet, Site, and Network Activity:  This includes text messages or emails sent to us, clickstream/online website tracking information, and data related to user activity (e.g., browser visits and cookies or other similar technologies).
  • Location Data:  we may receive information about your location and your mobile device, including a unique identifier for your device. In addition, in some instances, location information can be derived from your IP address or through your wi-fi connection.
  • Professional or Employment-Related Information: This includes occupation, title, employer, employment history, and education.

‍

While you are not obligated to provide us with any Personal Information, it is possible that some features of the Site may be inaccessible to you or that you may not be able to interact with the Site if you choose not to do so.

5. Online Tracking and Advertising

The Site may gather information about your activity through the use of tracking technologies. Information collected through these technologies is utilized for various purposes, including analyzing traffic on the Site and on third-party websites or mobile applications. The tracking technologies used include:

‍

Cookies - Cookies are pieces of information stored directly on the device you are using, allowing us to gather information such as the type of browser you use, the amount of time you spend on the Site, the websites or apps you visit, and any preferences you have set for your accounts. This information is used for business purposes, such as personalizing your experience, enhancing security, facilitating navigation, displaying content more efficiently, recognizing your device, and understanding how the Site is used.

‍

Other Technologies - We may also use other technologies, such as capturing screenshots while you use the Site, to understand how you navigate and use the Site. This information helps us to improve the Site by providing a better understanding of how it is used.

‍

Please note that if we combine any of the tracking data mentioned above with the Personal Information you provide, we will consider the combined information as Personal Information.

6. Other Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser or Device Information: Many browsers or devices collect information automatically, such as your MAC address, computer type (Windows or Mac), screen resolution, operating system name and version, device manufacturer and model, language, and Internet browser type and version. This information is used to ensure proper functioning of the Site.

It may be identified and logged in our server log files when you access the Site, along with the time of access and the pages visited. Collecting IP addresses is standard practice and is done by many websites and services. We use IP addresses to calculate usage, diagnose server problems, and manage the Site.
