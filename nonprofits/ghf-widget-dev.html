Mission Statement

The Georgian Heritage Foundation was founded in Santa Clara, CA in March 2020 with the sole purpose of preserving, promoting, and cultivating Georgian heritage in Silicon Valley. Their mission is to unite and strengthen the Georgian community and friends of Georgia, while honoring the beautiful country that is often called Saqartvelo.

Donate Share

Why donate?

The Georgian Heritage Foundation relies on the generosity of donors and supporters to help them achieve their mission of preserving and promoting Georgian culture, history, and traditions. Support from donors helps the organization continue to provide educational opportunities and cultural events to the community. For instance, donations help fund Sunday School programs that teach Georgian language, reading and writing, history, art, and poetry to children and teachers. Additionally, donations allow the organization to continue offering "Calligraphy as an Art" programs that received a grant from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. Lastly, support from donors also helps the organization to organize humanitarian aid to people suffering from Russian aggression, participate in Georgian cultural events, and provide cultural exhibitions of Georgian history and art.

The Georgian Heritage Foundation expresses their sincere gratitude to everyone who supports their cause and Sunday School. Without continuous support and encouragement, the organization would not have been able to come this far. Donors help the organization preserve and promote Georgian culture and heritage for generations to come.
