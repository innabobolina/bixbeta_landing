Mission Statement

Hellene Clavadistas, Inc. is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing a high level competitive diving training.
‍
Hellene Clavadistas, Inc., a 501(c)(3) organization, was founded in October 2016.
‍
Coach Stefanos has an extensive background both as a Diving and Gymnastics coach over the past 15 years.  Most recently he was awarded Regional Coach of the Meet for the 2016 UIL women’s diving and Coach of the Meet during the 2016 State of Texas UIL Women's championship meet in Austin.  His awards and accolades over his long career as a diver include 6 time World, 3 time European  and 4 time Pan American masters champion and numerous medals in international and world competitions. His love and passion for diving is evident in his coaching and mentoring of his divers.
‍
The Hellene Clavadistas started with eight divers and has grown to over twenty divers in five months with 7 qualifiers for the nationals competition! If you are interested in learning the art, grace, beauty and strength of Olympic style diving, contact us to schedule an evaluation.The Hellene Clavadistas was in the top American teams in the 2018 AAU National Championships, and some of the divers working with coach Stefanos have achieved to be National champions and earn Scholarships in the country's colleges.
