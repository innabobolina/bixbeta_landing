What is BixBeta solving for?

Organizations can achieve customer delight by offering crypto settlement services. However, cryptocurrencies are volatile in nature so firms often want to avoid holding crypto assets. BixBeta enables organizations to accept crypto transactions, but automatically converts them into the fiat currency of choice. Thus, without getting exposed to the the underlying risks of cryptocurrencies, firms can tap into the rapidly growing client market.

Who can open an BixBeta account?

To send money as an individual with BixBeta, you won't need an account. BixBeta is primarily designed for businesses and corporations to get embark on their crypto journey. Once you have created a corporate BixBeta account, you start off by customizing the plugin that fits your needs. Then copy the corresponding code from BixBeta onto your website or marketing campaign. And that's it. Your organization is ready to receive cryptocurrencies in two simple steps. Â 

How do I pay?

Our pricing model is simple. For each transaction, we combine a flat $0.99 fee with a dynamic 2.99% fee. For example, to accept a $100 donation (integrated into your accounting system), you would pay $3.98.

Additionally, BixBeta also offers a subscription and enterprise grade service tier that includes a custom terms and more flexibility for larger organizations.
